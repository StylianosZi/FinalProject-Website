    Please note-The NYCPA/NFPA membership term runs for one year from the date of membership payment. A 5% cancellation fee will be applied to all refunds. The cancellation fee will be deducted directly from the refund. No refunds will be granted after 30 days. Cancellation of the membership must be in writing and emailed to membership@nyc-pa.org with a copy to nycpaparalegals@gmail.com For questions or concerns, contact membership@nyc-pa.org.
    Group Membership is available for law firms, corporations, not-for-profit organizations and government agencies.

    1. 1-4 members - no discount
    2. 5-7 members - 10% discount
    3. 8-10 members - 15% discount
    4. 11+ members - 20% discount
